About

June 2021

Working on CubeRunner has not only been a great learning experience, but it's also been a great experience in general. I had lots of fun making this game, and now it's at its end of development. I don't think I will be working on any more updates for CubeRunner anytime soon, as I've added just about everything I had wanted to the game. However, I have plenty of ideas for exciting projects that lie ahead!

January 2021

CubeRunner is the first video game I've ever made. I'm currently a freshman in college as of writing this (January 2021), and I started this project over Winter Break. I'm excited to see what the future has in store for me, and I hope you all enjoy the game!
