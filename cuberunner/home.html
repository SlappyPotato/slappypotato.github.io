Synthwave 3D Endless Runner

CubeRunner is a 3D synthwave-themed endless runner game that features power-ups and game progression. As the Red Cube moves forward, you must steer left and right to avoid the obstacles, and it only gets faster as you go! Absorb power-ups to strengthen yourself against the obstacles, and collect valuable Bits to further upgrade those power-ups. You can enjoy more casual play in the Easy and Normal difficulties, or amp it up with Hard and Impossible!
